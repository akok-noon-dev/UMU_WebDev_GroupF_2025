1984

A chilling dystopian novel set in a totalitarian state where thought is controlled by the Party. It is a powerful warning about the dangers of unchecked political power and mass surveillance. A key text for political science and literature studies.

A satirical and futuristic novel presenting a society obsessed with technology, stability, and control through genetic engineering and conditioning. This classic challenges readers to consider the costs of technological progress and superficial happiness.
